Strangely, you are now in a primeval swamp, your boots soaked. It is as if you have stepped into another world, another time.

Where am I? You think. What epoch is this?

Silhouetted against a dark sky are large creatures, the sounds of their massive wings beating in the night air.

Something slithers between your boots.

A foul stench in the air.

In the distance, a rocky outcropping...

You will have to pass beneath the winged creatures above to reach it.
