You proceed, pistol in hand, wary of your surroundings...

until you find Adrian!

Ragged and afraid, he is huddled against a wall.

You guide him toward the door through which you entered this world...

only to find it hovering, untethered, in mid-air.

You pull him through, tumbling into the dank air of Abbott Hall.

You leave that wretched house and mount your horse, riding all night until you're home safe.

There, you live happily ever after...

though your adventures are far from over.
